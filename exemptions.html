Available Exemptions

Texas & Georgia — we file for all major exemption types

🤠 Texas Exemptions

🏠

Homestead Exemption

Primary residence exemption that removes a portion of your home's value from taxation. Available to all Texas homeowners.

Saves ~$1,973/yr average
👴

Over-65 Exemption

Additional exemption for homeowners age 65+. Includes a tax ceiling that freezes your school district taxes.

Additional $10K–$25K off value
♿

Disability Exemption

For homeowners who are disabled. Provides additional value reduction and a school tax ceiling similar to over-65.

Additional $10K off value + tax freeze
🎖️

Veteran / Disabled Vet

Exemptions for veterans with service-connected disabilities. 100% disabled vets get full property tax exemption.

Up to 100% tax exemption
🌾

AG / Timber (1-d-1)

Agricultural or timber use valuation. Land is taxed on productivity value rather than market value.

Can reduce value 90%+

🍑 Georgia Exemptions

🏠

Homestead (S1)

Standard homestead exemption for Georgia homeowners on their primary residence.

$2,000 off assessed value
👴

Senior (L5 — Age 65+)

Enhanced exemption for seniors age 65+ with income below threshold. Significant school tax reduction.

Up to $4,000 off for school taxes
♿

Disability

For totally and permanently disabled homeowners. Provides additional exemption on top of homestead.

Additional value reduction
🎖️

Veteran

Exemptions for disabled veterans and surviving spouses. 100% disabled vets receive full exemption.

Up to full tax exemption
